    Frequently Asked Questions

    Are you organic?

    We follow many organic practices, but we are not currently certified organic. Our cows are grass-fed and we avoid unnecessary antibiotics or chemicals.

    Are your cows free-range?

    Yes! Our cows graze outdoors in open fields for most of the year and are only brought indoors during extreme weather.

    Is your milk pasteurized?

    Yes. All milk from our vending machine is gently pasteurized on-site to ensure safety and freshness while preserving natural flavour.

    Do you use glass bottles?

    We offer reusable glass bottles, which you can purchase and refill from our vending machine. It's part of our commitment to sustainability.

    Where can I find your milk vending machine?

    Right beside our farm entrance on Distillery Road, Bushmills. See the map above for directions.
